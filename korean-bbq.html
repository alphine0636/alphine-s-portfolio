Korean BBQ Beef Short Ribs

CourseMain Dish
CuisineKorean
Prep Time24 hours (marination)
Cook Time20 minutes
Total Time24 hours 20 minutes
Servings4 servings
Calories431 kcal

Ingredients

  • 1 kg / 2 lb beef short ribs, butterflied Korean style or thinly sliced through the bone
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil (for cooking)

Marinade

  • 1/3 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp mirin
  • 1/4 cup onion, finely grated
  • 1/4 cup nashi pear, finely grated
  • 2 tsp garlic, finely grated
  • 2 tsp ginger, finely grated
  • 2 tbsp water
  • 1 tbsp toasted sesame oil
  • 1 tsp black pepper

Optional Garnishes & Sides

  • Green onion
  • Sesame seeds
  • Coriander/Cilantro leaves
  • Kimchi Fried Rice
  • Smashed Cucumbers

Instructions

  1. Make Marinade: Whisk all marinade ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Marinate Beef: Place beef in a ziplock bag or container. Pour marinade, coat well, cover and refrigerate for 24 hrs.
  3. Prepare to Cook: Remove beef from marinade. Discard leftover marinade.
  4. BBQ Method: Grill on high heat, 2 mins per side until caramelised. Rest under foil.
  5. Stovetop Method: Cut beef into skillet-sized pieces. Sear for 2 mins per side.
  6. Serve: Garnish and serve with sides.

Recipe Notes

  • Beef Cut: Korean-style ribs or oyster blade if unavailable.
  • Mirin Substitute: Use Shaoxing wine or skip.
  • Nashi Pear Substitute: Normal pear or red apple is fine.
  • Sesame Oil: Must be toasted (dark brown).
