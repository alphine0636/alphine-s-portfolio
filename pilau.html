Pilau (East African Spiced Rice)

Fragrant, beefy, perfectly spiced rice straight from East Africa. Trust ‚Äî this is a whole vibe. üçöüî•

CourseMain Dish
CuisineEast African
Prep Time20 minutes
Cook Time30 minutes
Total Time50 minutes
Servings5 servings
Calories366 kcal

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp cloves, 2 tbsp cumin seeds, 1/2 tbsp black peppercorns, 12 cardamom pods, 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 5 potatoes (quartered)
  • ¬º cup vegetable oil
  • 2 red onions (sliced)
  • 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 tbsp ginger (minced)
  • 1 serrano chile (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp pilau masala
  • 2 beef stock cubes
  • 1 lb beef sirloin (cubed)
  • 3 Roma tomatoes (diced)
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Roast spices lightly and grind them into Pilau Masala.
  2. Heat oil, fry onions until golden.
  3. Add garlic, ginger, serrano chile, cook until fragrant.
  4. Add beef, pilau masala, stock cubes, bay leaves, and salt. Brown the meat.
  5. Mix in tomatoes, cook till they release juices.
  6. Add potatoes and water. Boil for 10 mins.
  7. Stir in rice, cover tightly with foil. Cook on low for 20 mins.
  8. Fluff with a fork and serve hot with fresh Kachumbari!
